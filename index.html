Through his strong leadership, unwavering commitment, and tireless efforts, he led the struggle for the rights and freedoms of Muslims. Jinnah played a key role in founding Pakistan, which became a reality on August 14, 1947. His sacrifices, legal expertise, and inspiring speeches united millions and turned the dream of a separate homeland into a proud nation.
